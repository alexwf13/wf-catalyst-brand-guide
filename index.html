00 Tone & Positioning

Bold editorial confidence. Premium, warm, direct.

Not McKinsey-grey. Not SaaS-pastel. Not neo-brutalism/punk.

This is a $9,500 product for business owners aged 40–55 with high deal sizes. The design must feel trustworthy, precise, and quietly powerful — not loud or trendy.

Brand Architecture

Catalyst = independent identity, endorsed by Wonderflow.

Wonderflow appears only in the footer ("Delivered by Wonderflow FZ-LLC") and legal pages. Catalyst does NOT inherit Wonderflow's visual identity (unicorns, punk elements) — only shares the primary blue #198ED5.

02 Shapes & Radius

Radius: 0px everywhere. All cards, blocks, buttons, inputs.

Pill (full round): only tags, badges, chips.

No "slightly rounded" — this is a signature of the system.

03 Borders

Width: 3px    Color: #000000    Rule: Selective, NOT default

Where to Use 3px Border

✅Hero container
✅CTA button (always)
✅Guarantee block
✅1-2 key proof/artifact blocks per page

Where NOT to Use

❌Every feature card, FAQ item, divider, small card
❌Elements inside an already-bordered container

Screen limit: max 2-3 bordered blocks in one viewport.

Section dividers: 1px solid #989898 (or #EBEBEB on dark)

Hero / CTA / Proof3px solid #000
Regular cardNo border — use spacing

04 Shadows

Hard shadow: 6px 6px 0px #000000 — CTA buttons ONLY. Nothing else.

No soft shadows, blur, or glassmorphism anywhere.

Regular cards: separation through background and spacing.

08 Illustrations

Max 2-3 key illustrations per page: hero, proof section, after-day-15.

✅ Allowed

  • Isometric / line-art stylized artifacts
  • Product deliverable visualizations (Leak Map, Funnel Map, Tracker, Initiative Card)
  • Color: blue #198ED5 + black + accent from palette
  • Style: technical, information-forward, not decorative

❌ Forbidden

  • Characters, mascots, cartoon people
  • Abstract blobs, gradients, 3D spheres
  • Stock photos of people in offices
  • Emoji-style icons
  • Wonderflow unicorns / punk elements

09 What We Do NOT Do

❌Dark mode toggle — skip for v1. One confident light theme.
❌Gradients — everything is flat fill.
❌Glassmorphism, soft shadows, blur effects
❌Rounded corners anywhere except pill tags.
❌Borders on every card — max 2-3 per screen.
❌Hard shadow on cards — shadow is CTA-only.
❌Unicorns, monsters, comics, mascots — Catalyst is its own brand.
❌Hero font >80px — reads as youthful startup.
❌More than 1 accent color per block (except CTA).

10 Quick Reference

TL;DR for Designers

  • → Primary background: warm cream #FEFAEF, NOT white
  • → All corners: 0px (except pill tags)
  • → Black borders: 3px, but selective — not on every element
  • → Hard shadow: only CTA buttons
  • → Yellow: only CTA, never decorative
  • → Hero headline: 64-80px max, NOT 120px
  • → Page rhythm: cream (60-70%) → blue (1-2 sections) → dark (1-2 sections)
  • → Max 2-3 bordered blocks per viewport
  • → No gradients, no blur, no rounded UI, no stock photos
